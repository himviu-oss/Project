About

The story behind the stories.

Hi, I'm Norman

The Stray Dog Blog started the way most good things do - by accident. One dog at a market, one decision to stop, and suddenly a whole world opened up.

These pages are part journal, part field guide. I write about the dogs I meet on the street, the routines that keep colonies healthy, the science of behavior and welfare, and the slow, stubborn work of building trust with animals who have every reason not to give it.

I'm not a vet or a big organization. I'm one person with a truck, a feeding route, and a belief that small, consistent kindness adds up. If reading this makes you look twice at the next stray you pass, the blog has done its job.

Every dog has a story. Some of them just need someone to start writing it down.
